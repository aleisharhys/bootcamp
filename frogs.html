Fun Facts About Frogs

Cool Types of Frogs

  • Wallace's flying frog can glide from tree to tree.
  • The glass frog is completely translucent!
  • The golden poison dart frog is the most poisonous frog in the world!
  • Wood frogs have antifreeze properties; their hearts stop, and they're frozen but come back to life in warmer months!
  • The African clawed frog can regrow parts of its body, including heart, brains, and limbs!
